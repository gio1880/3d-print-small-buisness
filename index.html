Meet the Team

Support Us →
Premium Rice Cookers · FTC Robotics

We're Premium Rice Cookers, a student-run FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team based in Flushing, Queens, New York City. Every season we design, build, wire, and program a competition robot from the ground up — then take it head-to-head against teams from across the region.

Selling our 3D prints is how we fund the season, and it's also how we learn — real engineering, CAD, coding, and the business side of running a team. Every print you buy and every dollar you donate keeps students on the field, building and competing.
