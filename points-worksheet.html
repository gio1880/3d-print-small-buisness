A clicker is a keychain where every letter or symbol is its own clicky button. You pick the shape (straight row of 2–12, 2×2 grid, or 3×3 grid) and the letters.
Points = 80 + (20 × number of buttons)
Example: your 5-letter name in a row = 80 + 100 = 180 points.
